Lundin Sentinel Image-Hub

The hub contains Sentinel-1 images from Lundin's landing size in the Barents Sea. New images are generated twice a day. Satelite data are provided by The Copernicus Programme, directed by the European Commission.

By clicking on the Download-button under each image it is possible to download the original Sentinel data from Copernicus Colhub (MET NBS version) download site. Note that this data is password protected and requires a Colhub user. Click here to register a new Colhub account. Also note that these files can be very large and downloading raw data can therefore be time consuming.

Update interval: New images generated twice a day, at 8:00 AM and 8:00 PM (GMT)

Landing site location: 72°04'02.8"N, 21°48'19.8"E - Area 25km east/west and 50km north

Landing site area: Click here to view area in geojson format

Download source: NBS Copernicus Data Hub

Data manipulation: SNAP toolbox, developed by ESA (European Space Agency)

Manipulation operations: Calibration, Subsetting, Speckle Filtering and Range Doppler Terrain Correction

Wind information: yr.no - Goliat Platform observation site
